Unique Feature

Driving Scenario Simulations

The only Irish theory test app with real-time driving scenarios. A 60fps physics engine evaluates your decision-making at real road situations — not just your ability to memorise answers.

  • T-junction gap finding
  • Pedestrian crossing reactions
  • Roundabout entry timing
  • Narrow road oncoming traffic
  • Speed zone hazard recognition
  • Straight road merging and lane changes
  • Time-to-collision (TTC) feedback chart
  • XP rewards for decisive, safe decisions

Smart Learning

Spaced Repetition System

DTT Ireland uses an SM-2-inspired spaced repetition algorithm — the same proven technique behind Anki and Duolingo — to automatically resurface questions you get wrong at optimal intervals.

  • Adaptive intervals: 1 → 3 → 7 → 14 → 30 days
  • Wrong answers prioritised in review sessions
  • Up to 20 due questions per review session
  • Background sync via WorkManager
  • Streak reminders and daily challenge push notifications

Progress Tracking

Progress Dashboard & Achievements

Know exactly where you stand. The Progress tab shows your predicted exam score, per-category mastery, weekly study trend, and an XP streak system that keeps you coming back.

  • Daily streak counter and longest streak
  • Weekly study activity bar chart
  • 10 unlockable achievements (badges)
  • Bookmark questions for later review
  • Mistakes review after every session
