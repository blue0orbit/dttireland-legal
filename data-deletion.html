User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 24, 2026

If you have used Facebook Login to sign in to DTT Ireland Navigator, you may request deletion of all personal data we hold about you.

What Data We Hold

  • Your name and email address.
  • Your Facebook user ID (used solely to identify your account).
  • Your app preferences, saved journeys, and settings.
  • Usage analytics associated with your account.

How to Request Deletion

Option 1 – Delete from within the App:
  1. Open DTT Ireland Navigator on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete My Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
Option 2 – Email Request:
  1. Send an email to blue0orbit@gmail.com with subject: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include your registered email address or account name.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

Facebook-Initiated Deletion

  1. Go to your Facebook App Settings.
  2. Find DTT Ireland Navigator in connected apps.
  3. Click Remove. We will delete your data within 30 days.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account and all personally identifiable information will be permanently deleted.
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be traced back to you may be retained.
  • You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.
