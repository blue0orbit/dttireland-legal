User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 25, 2026

If you have used Google Sign-In or Facebook Login to sign in to Navigator: Ireland Driving Theory Test, you may request deletion of all personal data held about you at any time.

What Data Is Held

  • Your name and email address (received at sign-in from Google or Facebook).
  • Your profile picture URL (if provided by your sign-in provider).
  • Your Facebook or Google user ID (used solely to identify your account).
  • Your study progress: quiz results, exam scores, spaced repetition data, category accuracy, and overall readiness score.
  • Your gamification data: XP points, achievement badges, study streaks, and Driving Grade.
  • Your language preference and app settings.
  • Anonymised usage analytics associated with your account (screens visited, features used).
  • Crash and error reports linked to your device (via Firebase Crashlytics).

How to Request Deletion

Option 1 — Delete from within the App

  1. Open Navigator on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete My Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email Request

  1. Send an email to blue0orbit@gmail.com with the subject: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address or account name associated with your Navigator account.
  3. Receipt will be confirmed within 5 business days and deletion completed within 30 days.

Option 3 — Facebook-Initiated Deletion

  1. Go to your Facebook App Settings.
  2. Find Navigator: Ireland Driving Theory Test in your connected apps.
  3. Click Remove. This will trigger a deletion request and your data will be removed within 30 days.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account and all personally identifiable information will be permanently deleted.
  • All study progress, exam history, achievements, and streaks linked to your account will be removed.
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data that cannot be traced back to you may be retained for service improvement purposes.
  • A confirmation email will be sent when deletion is complete.

Contact

Questions? Email blue0orbit@gmail.com.
