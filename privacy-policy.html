Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

OVERVIEW

DTT Ireland is an independent study app created by an Irish developer to help learners prepare for the official Irish driving theory test. It has no affiliation with the Road Safety Authority (RSA) or any government agency.

If you have questions about how your data is handled, contact us at blue0orbit@gmail.com.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you sign in with Google or Facebook we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.

We store your study activity — quiz results, scores, streaks, XP, bookmarks, and achievement data — so your progress syncs across devices.

We also collect anonymised device and interaction data (device model, OS version, app version, crash reports) for diagnostics and app improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you personally.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Sync your progress across devices
  • Personalise your study recommendations
  • Deliver ads to free-tier users (ads are removed with a Premium upgrade)
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

DTT Ireland integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and analytics
  • Google AdMob — banner and interstitial ads (free-tier users only; GDPR consent managed via the Google UMP form shown on first launch)
  • Facebook Login — social sign-in
  • Unity Ads — rewarded video ads (free-tier users only)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases
  • Cloudflare R2 — media asset storage

Each provider processes only the data necessary for their specific function.

DATA PROTECTION & RETENTION

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Your data is shared only with the service providers listed above, and only to the extent required to operate the app.

Your account data is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days. You can request deletion at any time by contacting blue0orbit@gmail.com or using the Delete Account option in Settings.

YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to certain types of processing

This app is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under 16 has provided data, please contact us immediately at blue0orbit@gmail.com.

This policy complies with Irish and EU data protection law, including the Irish Data Protection Act 2018. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you via the app and give you the opportunity to review the updated terms before they take effect.
